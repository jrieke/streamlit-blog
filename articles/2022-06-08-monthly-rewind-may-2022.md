## **üèÜ App of the month üèÜ**

Our May featured app of the month is... ü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Å

[BERT Semantic Interlinking Tool](https://share.streamlit.io/searchsolved/seo/production_code/streamlit/bert_interlinker/bert_semantic_interlinker.py?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by [Lee Foot](https://leefoot.co.uk/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)!  
  
Upload an internal\_html.csv from Screaming Frog and discover pages to interlink. This app allows you to find related products and blogs, supporting content for category pages, and related pages to crosslink. It also lets you group similar pages. You can filter the source and the destination URL type, select cluster size and accuracy, and cluster any column. [[code](https://github.com/searchsolved/search-solved-public-seo/blob/main/bert_interlinker/bert_semantic_interlinker.py?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)]  
  
\*Learn about the [latest updates here](https://leefoot.co.uk/portfolio/bert-automatic-interlinking-streamlit-app-v2/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) as of Dec. 2023

## **Streamlit May updates**

A lot of exciting things happened in May! Let's take a look.

### **üîç Current release: 1.10.0**

The latest release is [1.10.0](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/version-1-10-0/25987/8?ref=streamlit.ghost.io). Recent updates include native support for multipage apps and redesigned `st.dataframe`. Be sure to check out the [changelog](https://docs.streamlit.io/library/changelog?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to learn more about the latest features and fixes.
