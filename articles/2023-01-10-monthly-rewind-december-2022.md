## ****üèÜ App of the** m**onth üèÜ****

Our December featured app of the month is... ü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Å  
  
[Feather AI](https://drhoffma-feather-ai-podcast-streamlit-main-er7mw2.streamlit.app/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by Josh Cadorette and David Hoffman.   
  
Feather AI is an audio-to-text summarizer app built in 1 week during a hackathon! To use the app, all you need to do is add a link to a podcast or YouTube video, enter your email, and get a polished summary in your inbox in 5-10 minutes. It can be used for many use cases, such as summarizing earnings calls, meetings, or webinars.

## **Featured community content**

Here are some great apps and tutorials by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Using GPT3 and DALL-E, Nikolas's [Generate Tweets](https://twitter.com/kinosal/status/1597686962546429952?s=20&t=qDxhBDYhhhGatM2Q6AQf5w&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app will create Tweets for you and upload them to your account!

* Learn about the streamlit-pandas component and how to [Easily Make DataFrame App with Streamlit Pandas (Only 2 lines of Python!)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L4KVn1XnSAA&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) from William.
* Experiment with Ultralytics Yolov5 models using Robin's [yolov5-ui](https://twitter.com/robmarkcole/status/1601229029314420736?s=20&t=W-tQQ6r7OKbIIzVMZIrigw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app.
* Ozgur's [Euro league stats](https://twitter.com/ozgurdugmeci/status/1601182591755128832?s=20&t=tYKAo8717-vPd2liX8EHXw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app decides the most productive 5 team members in selected matches.
* With Ali's [Cross Chain Monitoring Tool](https://twitter.com/AliTslm/status/1606240405053284353?s=20&t=1oyz-P2jZfDX1RyhZo61Iw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io), you can compare +10 blockchains in different sectors and view the performance of each blockchain.
* Indraneel created his [Curated](https://twitter.com/ineelhere/status/1602676527694598147?s=20&t=u-yueIbiBd8mgJUMy5P7Cg&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app to provide awesome resources for learning new skills.
* The [Psychiatric Medications Side Effects](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/nadezhda-filipenko_psychiatric-medications-side-effects-activity-7010554682678865920-crdo?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop) app by Nadya and team lets you input a person's psychiatric medications and conveniently displays all their side effects for clearer understanding.
* Get insights about the impact of 5G on the top-level management of Atliquo with Jegadheesh's [Atliquo Telecom - Performance](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jegadheesh-p_codebasics-codebasicsresumeprojectchallenge-activity-7008983435847364609-U5GS?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop) app.
* Listen to the [Streamlit Community Song](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C3LySQLM6n0&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by Tom John, with lyrics written by ChatGPT.
