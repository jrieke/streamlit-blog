## ****üèÜ App of the** m**onth üèÜ****

Our October featured app of the month is... ü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Å

[Streamlit-geospatial](https://streamlit.gishub.org/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by [Qiusheng Wu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/qiushengwu/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)!  
  
This multi-page web app demonstrates various interactive geospatial applications created with open-source mapping libraries such as leafmap, geemap, pydeck, and kepler.gl. Create timelapses, upload vector data, search basemaps, and more! This is an open-source project. [[code](https://github.com/giswqs/streamlit-geospatial?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)].

## **Featured community content**

Some great apps and tutorials by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Zakaria's [Clean Speech Audio with IBM Watson STT and Streamlit App](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MOy8XvooqEc&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) lets you clean up your Audio recordings by removing unwanted spaces and words
* Qiusheng incorporated [leafmap apps](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/qiushengwu_leafmap-streamlit-geospatial-activity-6850770085225857024-FiZJ/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to his open-source project
* In his video tutorial, PartTimeLarry showed how to use Streamlit to build a simple UI on top of [OpenSea NFT API](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=49SupvcFC1M&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Akshanh made a [Streamlit Playground](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/streamlit-playground-app/18118?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app where you can learn, experiment, and play with Streamlit components on the fly
* In the video [Quantum Tic Tac Toe with IBM Qiskit and Streamlit](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DeFzlOJeWI0&ref=streamlit.ghost.io), Jay showed how to build a Quantum version of the classic Tic Tac Toe game
* Loren created [Dashboard Evolution Covid19 en France](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/streamlit-app-for-for-monitoring-the-evolution-of-covid-19-in-france/18189?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to show different views of the evolution of the Covid-19 in France
* Jos√©'s [STL files from molecules](https://twitter.com/napoles3D/status/1451381633576222721?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app returns molecular models in STL format for 3D printing
* Sean created [xPlot](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/xplot-interactive-2d-and-3d-plotting-app/18422/4?ref=streamlit.ghost.io), a data explorer to plot data from files for analysis
* Find rankings, result types, and ranking URLs for a bulk list of keywords in Michael's [Keyword rankings](https://twitter.com/vdrweb/status/1452324025619845127?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app
