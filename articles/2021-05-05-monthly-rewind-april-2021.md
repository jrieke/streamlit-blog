## ****Featured community content****

Some great apps, repos, and articles by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Robert's [Distribution Analyse](https://twitter.com/robertdzudzar/status/1377801557459136513?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)r app allows you to interactively explore continuous distributions from SciPy and fit distribution(s) to your data
* Ethan walks through how he deployed his app in [Brewing a Coffee Recommender (Part 2)](https://ethan-feldman.medium.com/brewing-a-coffee-recommender-part-2-e82d557658d4?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Bhavesh shows how you can track prices of cryptocurrencies in his video tutorial [Bitcoin Tracking with 20 lines of Python code](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XVP3KaKbb7Y&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* In Misra's video, she goes into [How to Make Pie Charts in Streamlit Using Plotly](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rxWkIn1EZnM&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* The [Python Data Visualization Tour](https://twitter.com/discdiver/status/1379939287307841544?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app by Jeff visualizes plots and code for 6 Python plotting libraries at once: bokeh, altair, matplotlib, seaborn, plotly (express) & pandas
* Charly released a beta version of his app [StreamProphet](https://twitter.com/DataChaz/status/1382317842880663552?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) that lets you visualize your forecasted SEO traffic
* Another great tutorial from 1littlecoder shows how to [Build Streamlit Dashboard Template for Python Data Science | Tableau Alternative in Python](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tx6bT2Sh9R8&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* In his video [How to deploy ML app in 2 mins | Streamlit Sharing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_fE8maAS5C0&ref=streamlit.ghost.io), Anuj walks you through how to deploy your app on sharing
* Gareth made interactive character interaction graphs in his [Star Trek Script Analysis Dashboard](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/star-trek-script-analysis-dashboard/12266?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Aaron's app [NFL Mock Draft Database](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/aaroncolesmith_nfldraft-datavisualization-dataanalytics-activity-6793526366919901185-Vr72?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) determines probabilities of the draft results with nice visualizations
* Justin outlines how Reverie Labs is [Building Web Applications From Python Scripts with Streamlit](https://blog.reverielabs.com/building-web-applications-from-python-scripts-with-streamlit/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in his blog post
