## ****üèÜ App of the** m**onth üèÜ****

Our September featured app of the month is... ü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Å  
  
[Abstractonator](https://abstractonator.labs.crossref.org/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by Crossref Labs!  
  
This app is a game that tests if you can distinguish machine-generated content (MGC) from human-generated content (HGC). Try to guess which scholarly abstracts are the human-generated originals and which are AI-generated! [[code](https://gitlab.com/crossref/abstractonator?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)]

### **Featured community content**

Here are some great apps and tutorials by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Fanilo tackles a bunch of community questions in his video [Streamlit Pro answers YOUR questions & messages ‚Ä¢ 1k AMA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0mFwVBVu21c&ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Sephora's [NLLB-200 translator for lyrics](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/sephora-bemba_nllb-meta-machinelearning-activity-6970638397174341632-ZfRe?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop) demo app lets you translate lyrics from popular songs to 200 languages, including low-resource languages.
* Roll the dice and see the distribution of the sum of die results with Bruno's [Playing dice!](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/bruno-vieira-ribeiro-a1b1b259_streamlit-activity-6970608283418079232-odMc?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop) app.
* With Koen's [SEO A/B Test Analyzer](https://twitter.com/seobrein/status/1568587861267447811?s=20&t=uRVbIFP4H_MHB0GdnKJrog&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) you can validate almost all SEO changes and optimizations before they're implemented on your website.
* Valeria's [Tarot Reading App](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/valeriafilippou_tarot-reading-app-valeriafilippou-activity-6977194696536154113-tP00?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop) will give you a three-card spread on your past, present, and future.
* Use Mykola's app to discover [Land Surface Temperature - River Basins](https://twitter.com/MykolaKozyr/status/1569356530453204998?s=20&t=uRVbIFP4H_MHB0GdnKJrog&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in Europe and the USA.
* Nicholas attempts a fun coding challenge in his YouTube video: [I tried to build a Python Machine Learning Streamlit App in 7 Minutes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ebb4gUI2IpQ&ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Yuichiro's [stlite sharing](https://twitter.com/whitphx/status/1572083200293507073?s=20&t=RkVfNFF4MMownd4YcYbOrg&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) lets you write and share Streamlit apps online serverlessly!
* Sam's tutorial [Python-Based Data Viz (With No Installation Required)](https://towardsdatascience.com/python-based-data-viz-with-no-installation-required-aaf2358c881?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) walks you through setting up a Streamlit app to share with stlite.
* With Zakaria's [Distillation Column](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/zakaria-nadah-00ab81160_distillation-python-maccabthiele-activity-6978416633895653376-zC5Z?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop) app, you can estimate the required performance of a given system for a specified reflux rate and then use the values to help size a complete distillation column.
* In his YouTube tutorial, Fede teaches you [How to code and deploy a Streamlit App in less than 20 minutes!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trgC78f6T18&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Arvindra made a mini-tutorial app to help with [Initializing widget values and getting them to stick without double presses](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/mini-tutorial-initializing-widget-values-and-getting-them-to-stick-without-double-presses/31391?ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Arjune's first Streamlit app, [HR Analytics](https://twitter.com/bce3227/status/1575380690522079232?s=20&t=qIzbPXyACTlanJehhju8zA&ref=streamlit.ghost.io), visualizes a summary of each department and each employee in the organization.
* Use Michael's [Drug Target Prediction](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/prediction-of-new-drugs/31316?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app to find molecules with similar structural properties that can serve as a source for new drugs.
