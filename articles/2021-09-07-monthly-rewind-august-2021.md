## ****üèÜ App of the** m**onth üèÜ****

Our August featured app of the month is......ü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Å

[Parking Spot Vacancy](https://share.streamlit.io/rejexx/parkingspot_vacancy/main/src/streamlit_app.py?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by [Jeffrey Jex](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jeffreyjex/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io).

Wanna know how many parking spots are available at the Jackson Hole Wyoming Town Square before making the trip? This app takes a live stream of the area and and processes it with Mask R-CNN to detect cars and trucks. It then compares locations with parking places to see which are available. [[code](https://github.com/rejexx/Parkingspot_Vacancy?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)]

## **Featured community content**

Some great apps, videos, and articles by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

1. 1littlecoder showed how to make an app that can extract text from images in his video [Python Tutorial to build Image to Text App using EasyOCR & Streamlit](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j7TH0MRlnGs&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
2. Python Engineer created a [Stock Prediction Web App In Python In 60 Seconds!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=brGWvfBC6W0&t=6s&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in his short
3. Yaniss' [Broken Links Finder](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/yanissi_linkbuilding-python-activity-6832943772930768896-Loq-?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app takes a domain and finds every broken link in any of the pages present on the website
4. See how Streamlit is used for [Sharing micro-computed tomography(MCT) image analysis in dentistry](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/sharing-micro-computed-tomography-mct-image-analysis-in-dentistry/15974?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in Jongki's app
5. Avra gave tips on how to design your apps in his video [STREAMLIT Python TRICKS - make your WEB APP look BETTER in 6 Minutes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G9U4Uixssf0&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
6. [SEOgre](https://twitter.com/iPullRank/status/1427661275652366337?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by Colt, is a tool that lets you layer Google updates, GSC traffic, and SEO tool data exports to better analyze data
7. Jose made an app [SMILES + RDKit + Py3DMOL](https://twitter.com/napoles3D/status/1427324902197977088?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to learn about **S**implified **M**olecular **I**nput **L**ine **E**ntry **S**ystem
8. In his video [Streamlit Tutorial for Building Analytics Metric Dashboard in Python](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xHNXuyWAfCI&t=3s&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) 1littlecoder went over the new st.metric feature
9. Jenna created [Interact with Gapminder Data](https://twitter.com/JennaJrdn/status/1429892918458949632?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io), an app that students can learn how to make in her [workshop](https://carpentries-incubator.github.io/python-interactive-data-visualizations/index.html?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) on interactive data visualizations in python
10. Ahmed wrote an overview outlining [7 Reasons Why You Should Use the Streamlit AgGrid Component](https://towardsdatascience.com/7-reasons-why-you-should-use-the-streamlit-aggrid-component-2d9a2b6e32f0?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
11. Misra came back with another great YouTube tutorial showing [How to use Streamlit session states and callback functions | Make your apps remember things!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5l9COMQ3acc&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
