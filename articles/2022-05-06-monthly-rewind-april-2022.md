## **Featured community content**

Here are some great apps and tutorials by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Get insight into energy production and use Benedict's [Energytics](https://twitter.com/benthecoder1/status/1508474003425431554?s=20&t=Q4fKEH7EroWEtNzf1BoG1A&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app.
* Anna's [Hiking Upward Recommender](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/activity-6916123480207855616-Fvp2?utm_source=linkedin_share&utm_medium=member_desktop_web) app can help you find your next similar hike.
* Sharone's article shows how to [Make Dataframes Interactive](https://towardsdatascience.com/make-dataframes-interactive-in-streamlit-c3d0c4f84ccb?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by using Streamlit-Aggrid.
* In his [Introduction to Streamlit and Streamlit Components](https://auth0.com/blog/introduction-to-streamlit-and-streamlit-components/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io), Arvindra details how to build not only a Streamlit app but also custom components to expand its functionality.
* Christoph's [Prettymapp](https://twitter.com/chrieke/status/1511676062165520386?s=20&t=OLrgfbF_1f9dtNvwIY48vg&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app lets you create beautiful artistic maps in your browser for any address.
* Learn how to [add keyboard shortcuts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SLyS0v8br20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to navigate through the Streamlit apps with Fanilo's video tutorial.
* The [Striking Distance Creator](https://twitter.com/LeeFootSEO/status/1514956336446021633?s=20&t=0GygGGqFhDn7HQ_Oa2LiAw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app by Lee blends keyword & crawl data to provide actionable insights for keywords close to ranking.
* Japan created an app for cryptocurrency prediction called [CryptoBase](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/cryptobase-a-crypto-prediction-app/24159?ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Jina AI released [Jina NOW](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/jina-now-the-one-liner-neural-search/24717?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)â€”a no-code solution for creating a neural search with a built-in Streamlit interface!
* [spacy-streamlit](https://twitter.com/spacy_io/status/1514610098655485969?s=20&t=7cj9EQsYBevODSHr6KmQaw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) got an upgrade with improved NER visualization, UX and layout enhancements, and two new example scripts.
* Joan teaches how to [Deploy Machine Learning Web Applications with Streamlit](https://ngugijoan.medium.com/deploy-machine-learning-web-applications-with-streamlit-238b0380679d?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) and seamlessly interact with models in a creative and easy way.
* Okld released an awesome new component [Streamlit Elements](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/streamlit-elements-build-draggable-and-resizable-dashboards-with-material-ui-nivo-charts-and-more/24616?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) that allows you to build draggable and resizable dashboards with Material UI, Nivo charts, and more!
* Learn how to [Generate Images with Your Voice Using DALL-E](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fRa2rmDvOCY&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in Assembly AI's tutorial by Patrick.
