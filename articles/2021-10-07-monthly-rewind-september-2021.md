## **Featured community content**

Some great apps, videos, and articles by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Jesse's YouTube tutorial showed how to perform [File Downloads with Streamlit Download Button](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n37JoNMvxyE&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Data Professor chatted with Tyler about his new book on his podcast [Data Science Podcast with Tyler Richards (Facebook Data Scientist)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uPg7PEdx7WA&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Mohammad launched [DummyLearn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/mkhorasani_online-machine-learning-dummy-learn-activity-6838484692573016064-bv17?ref=streamlit.ghost.io), a free online machine learning platform
* Nelson created an app called [explainMyModel](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/nelson-ogbeide-013569171_classification-google-drive-activity-6842040241256189952-tSpt?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) that explains different models and their feature contributions
* [TeFIRE Calculator](https://twitter.com/lejimmy/status/1436361874501783577?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by Jimmy let you play with crypto projections and monthly income
* Spidy showed how to make a vaccination center and availability checker app in his video [Py-Cowin | Find Vaccination Center using Python Streamlit](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3tS2C4Q7jXc&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Pavan came out with another great CV app that lets you convert a [Video to GIF](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/video-to-gif-maker-with-streamlit/17297?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Marshall's app visualized [US State Migration](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/marshall-krassenstein_data-streamlit-python-activity-6844258790930550784-zWiv?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) data from 2019
* Data Professor answered the question [Is it Possible to Add a Navigation Bar to Streamlit Apps?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hoPvOIJvrb8&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in his video tutorial
* Teddy created a fun [PvP Gomoku](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/pvp-gomoku-game-in-streamlit/17403?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) game app
* Tunahan wrote a tutorial [Control the Flow: Streamlit](https://wilfroy.medium.com/control-the-flow-streamlit-2911e65caaa9?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) showing how to build an app and use session state
* Harshit came out with a popular video tutorial [Building a buzzing stocks news feed using NLP and Streamlit | Named Entity Recognition & Linking](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G5ycs1hFSKk&ref=streamlit.ghost.io)
* Avra's [SciLit](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/scilit-scientific-literatures-and-analytics-search-app/17547?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app let you search through scientific publications based on keywords
* Lucas and his team created a clustering strategy app [iRaPCA clustering](https://twitter.com/capigol/status/1439923545698488321?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io), with downloadable validation metrics and graphs
