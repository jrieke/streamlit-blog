## ****üèÜ App of the** m**onth üèÜ****

Our March featured app of the month is... ü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Å

[Rascore](https://share.streamlit.io/mitch-parker/rascore/main/src/rascore/rascore_gui.py?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by Mitchell Parker and Roland Dunbrack!  
  
Rascore is a tool for analyzing structures of the RAS protein family. It can also be used to build an updatable database of all available RAS structures in the Protein Data Bank. This app aims to simplify the biological study of RAS proteins in cancer and other diseases and facilitate RAS drug discovery. [[code](https://github.com/mitch-parker/rascore?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)]

## **Featured community content**

Here are some great apps and tutorials by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Avra demonstrated how to use [STREAMLIT with AG Grid Table - Interactive Table](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F54ELJwspos&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in his YouTube video.
* In his article, James went over [How to Create Custom Streamlit Components](https://towardsdatascience.com/how-to-create-custom-streamlit-components-de6a00a7d5ab?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)!
* Andrea built an app for [Web Scraping Made Easy](https://twitter.com/cyberandy/status/1499798477068554243?s=20&t=OB0oJHy64utXd275F01ayw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) using the AutoScraper library.
* Qiusheng added an interactive [Split-Map](https://twitter.com/giswqs/status/1503358991434489858?s=20&t=mzWck_chC-NdZu7l5_YrHQ&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) feature to his geospatial app.
* Alan detailed how he used Streamlit for [Visualizing the Economic Impact of the Pandemic](https://towardsdatascience.com/visualizing-the-economic-impact-of-the-pandemic-using-jupyter-notebooks-and-streamlit-c25c91411d1e?ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Gerard created an app to explore [Interactive Timeseries Forecasting with Darts](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/interactive-timeseries-forecasting-with-darts/22914?ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* [Fidelity Account Overview](https://twitter.com/GarsBar35Plus/status/1505765323995156483?s=20&t=1TJ6rFDbInWwnrtKuQTwyg&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) is another app from Gerard that visualizes your Fidelity data.
* In an AssemblyAI YouTube video, Misra showed how to make an app for [Auto-generating meeting notes with Python](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RMBhwwqeDOw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Enter a keyword and see the list of Google questions with Mihir's [Realtime - People Also Ask Tool](https://twitter.com/mihir23192/status/1507561183091511298?s=20&t=Q4fKEH7EroWEtNzf1BoG1A&ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
