## ****üèÜ App of the** m**onth üèÜ****

Our June featured app of the month is... ü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Åü•Å

[Learning log](https://tylerhillery-learning-log-streamlit-app-p6ig3y.streamlitapp.com/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by Tyler Hillery!   
  
Inspired by GitHub's contribution graphs, Tyler created this app to track the time he spends on learning. You can see the date of the learning session, the length of time, what was studied and where, along with his contextual notes. The app keeps Tyler accountable to his learning goals and helps visualize his progress! [[code](https://github.com/TylerHillery/learning-log?ref=streamlit.ghost.io)]

## ****Streamlit** June u**pdates****

A lot of exciting things happened in June! Let's take a look.

### ****üîç Current** r**elease:** 1.10.0**

The latest release is [1.10.0](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/version-1-10-0/25987/8?ref=streamlit.ghost.io). Recent updates include native support for multipage apps and the redesigned `st.dataframe`. Be sure to check out the [changelog](https://docs.streamlit.io/library/changelog?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to learn more about the latest features and fixes.

### ****üîÆ** Upcoming**

These new features are currently on the horizon:

* Unique and custom subdomains
* Replaying of cached `st.` calls
* User-resizable `st.sidebar`

Check out our [roadmap app](https://roadmap.streamlit.io/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to see what else we're working on. ü•≥
