## ****Streamlit** August u**pdates****

Take a look at these updates and highlights from August!

### ****üîç Current** r**elease:** 1.12.0**

The latest release is [1.12.0](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/version-1-12-0/29078/21?ref=streamlit.ghost.io). Recent updates include improved built-in charts, resizable side bar, and the ability to put static elements into functions that are cached via `st.experimental_memo` or `st.experimental_singleton`. Be sure to check out the [changelog](https://docs.streamlit.io/library/changelog?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to learn more about the latest features and fixes.

### ****üîÆ** Upcoming**

These new features are currently on the horizon:

* Unique and custom subdomains
* Ability to hide space for an empty label
* Apps are indexed by Google

Visit our [roadmap app](https://roadmap.streamlit.io/?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to see what else we're working on. ü•≥

### **Featured community content**

Here are some great apps and tutorials by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Avery walks you through how to make a [Sports Analytics Project With Python & Streamlit](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VuDtobSr8IE&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) that will look great on your data science portfolio.
* Learn from Jesse how to [Build a Static Code Analysis App with Streamlit and Python](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIMue_3DY_4&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in his YouTube tutorial.
* Timotius' app visualizes various aspects of [Gender Equality in Indonesia](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/timotius-marselo_dataanalytics-datavisualization-python-activity-6961214957887660032-BVuH?utm_source=linkedin_share&utm_medium=member_desktop_web).
* Explore the data of the [Vietnam National Team](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/daryl-thai-dao-9a25751b1_github-darylgouilardvietnamdataapp-code-activity-6961666320127979520-5RAw?utm_source=linkedin_share&utm_medium=member_desktop_web) with Daryl's app.
* Eugenia teaches you [How to build a Web App to Transcribe and Summarize audio with Python](https://towardsdatascience.com/how-to-build-a-web-app-to-transcribe-and-summarize-audio-with-python-dc719cb9e1f5?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in her article.
* The Agriculture, Water, and Climate Group at the University of Manchester created [AquaPlan](https://medium.com/@agwater/aquaplan-a-new-interactive-tool-for-agricultural-water-management-and-climate-risk-assessment-82c50cb10144?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to enable farmers, businesses, and governments to make more informed decisions about water.
* Add a [Custom Streamlit Background Image/Color Gradient through CSS](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pyWqw5yCNdo&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) to your apps by following Fanilo's video tutorial.
* Get quick overview of your Netflix data with Sebastian's [Netflix Profile Analysis](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/netflix-gdpr-analysis-app/29564?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app.
* Generate unique QR codes with advanced customization using the [Flash QR](https://twitter.com/GarsBar35Plus/status/1572035245263982593?s=20&t=9Yk0PkfyjiANE0fPQHSAbw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app by Gerard.
* With William's [Shakespeare Search Engine](https://twitter.com/wjb_mattingly/status/1562484153333194753?s=20&t=2mCpNqBix9TUYYP8vIuHgw&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app, you can semantically search the works of Shakespeare.
* Learn and share more about your personality by taking the tests in Rinku's [Findself](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rinku-chhokar_hello-everyone-i-always-wanted-to-make-a-activity-6968785622224261121-13H0?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop) app.
