## **Featured community content**

Here are some great apps and tutorials by the Streamlit community. Check out the [Streamlit forum](https://streamlit.ghost.io/p/efb2b033-a8e4-47ec-bb60-f1313d84052a/discuss.streamlit.io) for even more community content.

* Greg's [Google Trends For Top GSC Keywords](https://twitter.com/GregBernhardt4/status/1456381141116665856?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app lets you label your top-performing GSC keywords with Google Trends.
* William gives a tutorial on how to build [An App to Update Excel File with Pandas and Streamlit](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LZH_7PCpN2o&ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Analyze the sales dynamics of an NFT collection published on the Algorand blockchain in Vilijan's [NFT Analytics App](https://discuss.streamlit.io/t/nft-analytics-app/18934/6?ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Bek teaches how to create a [Stock Prices Web App with Streamlit Framework](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e_OPmGjkAVs&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in his informative YouTube video.
* Fanilo's [Twittorial: Using Seaborn in Streamlit](https://twitter.com/andfanilo/status/1458426720256765959?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) gives a quick breakdown on plotting Seaborn plots in Streamlit.
* Bogdan recorded a demo of his awesome [Stock Beta Calculator](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rpEvRFftIVM&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app.
* Kabilan's [Mask Detection and Social Distancing Detection](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kabilan-n-908457191_github-kabilan-nmask-detection-and-social-distancing-activity-6865958748754706432-BBpw?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) app finds COVID risk in an area based on live video input.
* In his video tutorial, Siddhardhan shows how to [Deploy Machine Learning Model using Streamlit in Python](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WLwjvWq0GWA&ref=streamlit.ghost.io).
* Rahman's [DataCo Smart Supply Chain Dashboard](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rahman-firmansyah-79283512b_streamlit-highlight-activity-6868573275891613696-3-AT?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) provides nice graphics for data visualization.
* [GPT-3 for SEO](https://twitter.com/cyberandy/status/1463461575889670146?s=20&ref=streamlit.ghost.io) by Andrea is an app to test all the possible SEO use-cases for GPT-3.
* Learn how to [Build a Personal Webpage with Streamlit](https://medium.com/geekculture/build-a-promotional-webpage-with-streamlit-4088b87354d2?ref=streamlit.ghost.io) in Alan's article.
